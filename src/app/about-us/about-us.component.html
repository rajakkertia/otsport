About Us
WE ARE LIFE COACHES The inception of OT Sportmanagement stems from a commitment to enhance access to top-notch services and products for Georgian clubs and football players. Our mission is to elevate the organizational culture of clubs by streamlining, expediting, and amplifying the transparency and efficiency of their internal processes. Through a range of services and products, players are empowered to evolve more rapidly, align with European standards, and showcase their capabilities at an international level, fostering a pathway to success.
